# Overview

Permission Risk Heatmap (PRH) helps administrators and business reviewers identify permission exposure in SharePoint, prioritize risk, and move through remediation with a clear audit trail.

</figure>

## Purpose

PRH exists to make permission risk visible before it becomes a governance incident. It gives teams a structured way to review access exposure, validate business need, and track what changed across review cycles.

## Business Value

- Reduce time spent finding over-permissioned content.
- Prioritize the most important risks first using a consistent severity model.
- Support safer remediation through approval and business validation.
- Preserve evidence for governance reviews, audits, and follow-up actions.

## Intended Audience

- Administrators running recurring permission-risk reviews.
- Site owners and business reviewers validating whether access is still appropriate.
- End users who need to understand why a finding exists or why access changed after remediation.

## Core Capabilities

- Scope-based analysis across selected SharePoint lists and libraries.
- Severity-led findings review.
- Guided remediation and archive workflow.
- History and export support for audit evidence.
